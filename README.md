Hashtag Hashtag Thoughts

to build this project, first I was simply going to use YTDLP to fetch the subtitles or the extraction from the YouTube videos that are already available as the captions and YTDLP can extract them, but then I thought that just the captions would not be such a great data point to serve as context for the kind of questions a creator might be asking. A creator might be asking the agent more about the video. The creator doesn't only read the caption, he is looking at the videos, looking at the animations, the camera, the body language, the cues, etc. And if we just create a transcript or a dialogue caption there, we are losing a lot of rich data that we have in the process. So, instead of just doing a standard extraction like that, I thought it would be much much better if we could use Gemini which is a multimodal model. So, it has both video and audio understanding and we can give the complete video and audio to Gemini to then analyze that video and then create analysis transcription of it which can which is a much higher quality data point to index which the creator when he will chat can do and ask much more things about and understand much more deeper of what has been happening. So, to do that, I scrapped the initial idea of the transcription as just the subtitle or the caption. I am doing a much more in-detailed multimodal analysis of how a creator looks at other creators' video to better his own work or how he will basically analyse and improve his own work when he is looking at content from other creators in that sense. So, that is the first example, that is why the transcript that I am making is in itself a multi-workflow thing where the transcript would be much more unexpected in the way that the transcript is made and that I will share.

So I have a rich video transcription dot MD that basically tells how rich transcription is supposed to look like. Now for this is again much more customized and tailor-made for our use case where we will be using the structured generation that Gemini will give us by default to our advantage. To use meta data to use pre search filtering and then use hybrid search and then do some post processing as well for our re-ranking after all the retrievals are done. We are doing this specifically to reduce the hallucination. so that is about the the rack pipeline.
